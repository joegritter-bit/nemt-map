# Standard Operating Procedure: NEMT Dispatch Dashboard

**Document Type:** Internal Tool Access & Usage Guide
**Department:** Dispatch & Routing (Illinois Operations)
**Access Link:** [NEMT Dispatch Dashboard](https://joegritter-bit.github.io/nemt-map/dashboard.html)

---

## 1. Overview
The **NEMT Dispatch Dashboard** is our proprietary web-based analysis tool. Originally developed as our custom NEMT scraper and analysis bot, this web version provides our dispatch team with deep, automated insights into available marketplace trips to maximize routing efficiency and profitability.

### Core Benefits
* **Marketplace Analysis:** Automatically evaluates available trips to highlight the most viable options for our daily schedule.
* **Data-Driven Decisions:** Removes the guesswork from trip selection by providing custom analytics tailored to our specific operational metrics.
* **Strategic Planning:** Gives dispatchers a comprehensive, bird's-eye view of regional demand to better position our drivers.

---

## 2. Dashboard Features & Analysis Sections

### The Live Map (Top Right Corner)
Clicking the **Live Map** button opens an interactive, real-time map displaying all currently available trips across the marketplaces. 
* **Filtering & Toggles:** You can easily toggle specific days on and off, or overlay potential routes to see how trips align geographically.
* **Trip Details:** Click on any individual trip icon directly on the map to instantly reveal the full details, requirements, and rates for that specific trip.

### Data & Analysis Sections
* **Available Shifts / Opportunity Stats:** Summary panel showing number of qualifying shift opportunities, total estimated revenue, average revenue per hour, and current regular rider alerts to quickly gauge overall market strength.
* **Regular Rider Addresses:** High-priority alert list for recurring regular riders in the area, flagged as immediate dispatch opportunities when present.
* **Springfield Dispatch:** Dedicated table of Springfield-area route recommendations with job count, shift length, revenue metrics and route details for local operational planning.
* **Clinic Routes (Multi-Load):** Multi-stop clinic-focused route recommendations showing hub, job count, shift duration, per-hour revenue, total value, and top county area.
* **Traditional Routes:** Standard route recommendations for the core market, with performance metrics and route descriptions to support day-of dispatch decision-making.
* **Driver Schedule Extensions:** Current list of available driver extension windows for friends/fleet, showing driver name, day, broker, route and price for gap-filling opportunities.
* **Active Hotspots:** Real-time top pickup locations by volume (active trips in the last 2 hours) with broker insight and count of open trips.
* **Weekly Recurring Patterns:** Identified weekly recurrence patterns in trip data (day/time/pickup location), useful for building reliable routes and dispatch forecast planning.

## 3. Access & Daily Workflow

**Access Instructions:**
1. Navigate to the [NEMT Dispatch Dashboard](https://joegritter-bit.github.io/nemt-map/dashboard.html).
2. **Bookmark the Page:** For quick daily access, click the star icon in your URL bar.

**Daily Workflow:**
1. **Initial Review:** Begin your shift by opening the Dispatch Dashboard to review the custom analysis of current marketplace trips. 
2. **Trip Selection:** Evaluate the analyzed trips and the Live Map against our active driver schedules to determine which trips make the most logistical sense.
3. **Proceed to Route Building:** Once optimal trips are identified, transition to your standard dispatch screen. Use the **NEMT Rate Decoder extension** to click **+route** on those specific trips and compile them in the Route Builder application.
