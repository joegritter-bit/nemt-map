Exercise Protocol

🚶 Aerobic Days — 30 Min Walk or Bike
5–8pt systolic drop. Raises HDL, lowers triglycerides. Every other day minimum.
🧱 Isometric Days — Wall Sits / Static Squat Holds
4 sets × 2 min hold / 2 min rest. 8–10pt systolic effect. Progress: wall sit → freestanding static squat hold (harder, more muscle recruitment, stronger BP signal).
🫁 IMST — Daily (5 Minutes)
30 breaths at 75% max resistance. Do every morning regardless of aerobic/isometric day. Independent BP pathway — 9pt systolic in studies.
✓ Air squats = aerobic/isotonic. Wall sits / static holds = isometric. Different mechanisms — both useful on their respective days.
⚠️ No exercise within 24 hrs of screening day.

Avoid Entirely — 2 Week Window

Coffee / Caffeine
Already stopped. Full cessation = 8–12pt baseline drop vs 4–6pt day-of skip. Transition via green tea if withdrawal hits.
Alcohol
Raises BP + triglycerides. Full elimination for 2 weeks.
THC / Marijuana Gummies
Disrupts REM sleep, raises resting heart rate, sympathetic nervous system activation with habitual use. Undermines your entire evening recovery stack. Eliminate for 2 weeks especially 3–4 days before screening.
NSAIDs (Ibuprofen, Aleve, Aspirin)
Can raise BP. Skip aspirin despite having it in your stack.
Sodium over 1,500mg/day
Read every label. No processed or restaurant food. Swap salted pumpkin seeds to unsalted.
Sugar / Refined Carbs (day before screening)
Triglycerides are highly sensitive 24–48hrs before labs. Eat completely clean the day before.
Decongestants / Nicotine
Both vasoconstrictive — directly raise BP acutely.
